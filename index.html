Our Story

Wright Winery has developed into a vibrant and dynamic center of quality wines and entertainment. The family vibe can be felt immediately when stepping foot on the beutiful grounds right in the heart of Temecula Wine Country. It's the kind of place where you just release a deep sigh because you feel at home. Winery Wright also hosts many weddings, private events, and many other activities throughout the year.

Wedding in Winery Wright

With rolling green hills, endless vineyards and vista views, delicious wine and cuisine, and world-renowned wedding venues – the Wright Winery is the perfect place for a destination wedding. Wedding guests will be greeted with the warm hospitality that has made Wright Winery everything it is today. A private staircase leads you onto your own private dining level, exclusively reserved for your event. A glass of Sparkling wine would be the perfect accompaniment on the terrace as guests arrive from your off-site ceremony, or await the wedding ceremony on the adjoining deck.
